Who I am

There are three things in life that I value highly:

People

Every person has a story to tell. I enjoy listening to people and to recognize our similarities and differences. By identifying ourselves and our relations to others, we can achieve a common understanding, which is vital for a peacuful society.

Nature

Nature brings peace to the mind. By respecting nature and by challenging the elements, nature is an infinite resource for personal development, whether it is through physical challenges or by means of reflection.

Technology

Technology demonstrates human greatness. It is what we as the human race have accomplished together. Personally, it represents an exercise for the brain. It is easy to achieve flow when working with a technical problem.

What I do

I work as a Front End Developer and Co-founder at Wopify.

My interests

I like design, philosophy, technology and photography. You can also find me playing any kind of instrument or strolling around in nature for no apparent reason.
